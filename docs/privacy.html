Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026 | Last Updated: February 9, 2026 | Version 1.2.0

1. Introduction

ADappvark Toolkit ("we," "our," "us," or the "Company") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

Our Core Privacy Principles

  1. Data Minimization: We collect only the absolute minimum data necessary to provide our Services.
  2. Local-First: We store data locally on your device whenever possible.
  3. Transparency: We are clear about what we collect and why.
  4. No Tracking: We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or profiling.
  5. User Control: You maintain control over your data at all times.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Wallet Address: Your Solana wallet public address when you connect your wallet. Used for payment verification and transaction processing.
  • Consent Records: Timestamp of your acceptance of Terms of Service, stored locally for legal compliance.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data (for Sanctions Compliance): Approximate geographic location determined via GPS/network location (if permitted), SIM card country, network carrier country, or device locale. Used solely to verify you are not in a blocked jurisdiction. Location is checked at app launch and before each payment transaction. Not stored after verification - only the result (allowed/blocked) is retained.
  • Device and Package Information (Local Only): List of installed applications. This data never leaves your device.
  • App Usage Information (Local Only): Subscription status and preferences. Stored locally, not transmitted externally.

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • No Personal Identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • No Device Identifiers (IMEI, Android ID)
  • No Private Keys
  • No Analytics or Crash Reports
  • No Advertising Trackers
  • No Photos, Contacts, or Media Access

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or monetize your Personal Data.

Limited disclosure occurs only for:

  • Blockchain transactions (public by nature)
  • Legal requirements (court orders, etc.)
  • Business transfers (with notification)

5. Third-Party Services

  • Solana Blockchain: Payment processing (public transactions)
  • Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter: Secure wallet communication
  • Shizuku: System operations (local only)
  • Solana dApp Store: App reinstallation

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All App data is stored locally on your device
  • Protected by Android's application sandboxing
  • Deleted when you uninstall the App
  • We do not operate servers that store your data

7. Geographic Restrictions

The App is not available in 54 blocked jurisdictions including countries subject to comprehensive sanctions (Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Russia), significant restrictions (Belarus, Venezuela, Myanmar, Nicaragua, Eritrea), targeted sanctions, and FATF/AML high-risk designations. The complete list is provided in our Terms of Service.

Location is verified using multiple methods (GPS, SIM, network carrier, locale) at app launch and before each payment. If your location cannot be verified, access to the App will be restricted.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: View all data via App Settings
  • Deletion: Uninstall to delete all local data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the App

GDPR, CCPA, and other regional rights are fully supported.
